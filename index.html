Angels of Heart

We are a non-profit committed to helping the homeless, elderly and children of Nashville and El Salvador.

Who Are We?

Angels of Heart is a Christian organization based in Nashville, TN. We hold several events each year to benefit the forgotten people: the elderly, the homeless, and the children of our communities.

Get Involved

We have three major events that we create and manage each year. As we are able to scale as an organization, we will have additional or ongoing activities for assisting those in need. Details for each event can be found on our Facebook Page.

If you would like to find out about our ongoing needs and volunteer opportunities, please subscribe to our mailing list.
