ANGELS OF HEART

Angels of Heart is a Christian non-profit organization committed to helping forgotten people in the name of Jesus Christ. We are based in Nashville, Tennessee, and hold special events each year to benefit those in need.

Who are the forgotten people?

The elderly population, children and the homeless. We are also helping internationally with needy families in El Salvador. Our current projects are: Collecting items to create "survival kits" for homeless people and, Fundraising for our mission trip to El Salvador. In October we will begin collecting gifts for needy elderly in Nashville.
